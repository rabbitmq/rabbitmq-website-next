Commercial Services

Commercial Licensing, Support

VMware offers a range of commercial support and services for RabbitMQ:

  • Commercial support for the most recent open source release of RabbitMQ, available for a subscription fee.
  • Commercial releases of RabbitMQ, available for a subscription fee.

Commercial Releases of RabbitMQ

Commercial RabbitMQ edition includes all of the features of the open source version under a commercial license. Pricing is available here.

RabbitMQ for VMware Tanzu

VMware Tanzu™ RabbitMQ® for VMs gives developers self-service access to on-demand RabbitMQ clusters running on VMware Tanzu™ platform (formerly known as Pivotal Cloud Foundry).

RabbitMQ for Kubernetes

Tanzu™ RabbitMQ® for Kubernetes simplifies the operational experience to make RabbitMQ available on a Kubernetes-based platform, based on the RabbitMQ Operator for Kubernetes.

Training

The following companies provide free, virtual, or instructor-led courses for RabbitMQ: VMware, Erlang Solutions and LearnQuest. These training courses cover learning messaging with RabbitMQ, use cases, patterns, best practice design and architecture.

Consulting

Erlang Solutions, XPlatform Consulting, NobleProg, AceMQ, CSS Commerce, Visual Integrator, and others provide professional consulting services to address specific issues such as on-site troubleshooting, design review, deployment planning, project delivery and other value-adding services tailored to suit your needs.

Contacts for Commercial Support or Services

For any questions about the commercial licensing, support, consulting training services listed in this guide, please reach out to the vendors or see the contact us page.

Getting Help and Providing Feedback
